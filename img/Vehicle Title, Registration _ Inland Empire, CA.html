About Us

First and foremost, our hours are what make us unique from other registration services. Satzfyd Title Services is available for appointments only during weekday evenings between 5 to 9 pm and weekends from 9 am to 3 pm. Appointments are in a private environment where your conversations and information are kept confidential.

Second, we know the lingo and the scenarios of DMV, and will do our best to minimize the headache and hastle. Our services include various registration and title transactions for passenger, truck, trailer, motorcycle, OHV, and water vessels. We complete mobile VIN inspections for automobiles, trailers, and commercial tractors. And we also reissue substitute titles, registrations, and stickers. Need help with non-resident title transfers, dropping an old lienholder, private party transfers, salvage or junk certificates.

Satzfyd Title Services owner, Chelle Epperson, is a retired title clerk, Asst. Supervisor and Branch Manager. We've been behind the counter and the frustrated DMV customer, so we have your situation covered. Twenty six years of experience has given us the knowledge to tackle any title problem.

Contact us for your personalized consultation and we can help you complete the necessary DMV forms.

Providing you peace of mind that your business is in good hands.
